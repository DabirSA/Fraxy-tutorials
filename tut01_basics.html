- Basic Gameplay -

Fraxy is a top-down shoot-em-up, or shmup, game. The player is able to move and rotate freely on a 2D plane.

The player has weapons. Weapons use energy. Not dying also uses energy

Several ships, different stats, different weapon behaviour

Bosses, destructible parts, shoot core
